4. Programme delivery

What we need to know

This section contains questions 5 to 12. We will ask you about:

  • activities proposed
  • programme for mobilisation and delivery
  • approach to partner and stakeholder management
  • facilitating and nurturing stakeholder relationships
  • complainant journey
  • key success factors – outputs and outcomes
  • technical proposal
  • capturing and verifying data

Include information about your organisation and any consortium members.

Describe the activities you are proposing for the project and why.

Include details on:

  • the marketing approach
  • key milestones for delivery
  • the use of varied and digital platforms
  • any staffing requirements

(Max 1000 words)

Outline the key milestones for the implementation of your project.

This should include principal activities and milestones, with a particular focus on demonstrating the time needed to mobilise and bring the services into operation.

(Max 500 words)

How will you establish a network of local and national partners, and use these to help deliver outcomes?

For example, partners may help to promote the service, build confidence, encourage engagement, support complainants, provide data and insight.

(Max 500 words)

How will you build effective and trusted working relationships with the government, other public bodies, and other faith and belief groups?

Include:

  • a summary of previous work undertaken and the outcomes
  • evidence of collaborative working with stakeholders
  • key organisations you intend to form strong working relationships with

(Max 500 words)

Describe the anticipated experience for an individual wishing to report an anti-Muslim hate incident and the support they might expect to receive.

Include:

  • detail of how complainants will be able to submit reports
  • how reports will be verified
  • what support will be made available to victims

(Max 500 words)

Describe your proposed key performance indicators and detail the expected outcomes of your programme.

This includes:

  • the process you have for measuring success
  • how you would manage and improve the service to ensure that it remains effective
  • how the government would expect to be able to monitor and evaluate your progress

(Max 500 words)

What technical systems need to be adopted for the reporting service and how will they be managed?

Describe the systems you will implement and how data will be treated securely and in accordance with the data protection acts.

(Max 500 words)

Describe the type of data you will collect from complainants, and how it will be collected and verified.

(Max 500 words)
