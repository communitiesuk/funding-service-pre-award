What we need to know

Confirm that you have read and agree with the following declarations.

  • I am content that the household understands the commitment and importance
  • I agree to maintain regular and ongoing communication with the household as their property progresses
  • I have not and will not make any applications to alternative funding schemes for these adaptations that could be considered duplicate funding (for example, the Exceptional Costs Fund or Disabled Facilities Grant)
